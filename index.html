About Strickly Cocktails

Strictly Cocktails, based in Lusaka, Zambia, is a remote cocktail delivery service that brings incredible and refreshing libations right to your doorstep.

Their expertly crafted fruit cocktails are bursting with flavor and made with the finest ingredients. From classic favorites to unique creations, Strictly Cocktails offers a delightful experience that will leave you craving more.

How we began

Ms. Precious Kapesa, driven by her passion for mixology, founded Strictly Cocktails in Lusaka, Zambia.

Through her dedication to crafting exceptional cocktails and using premium ingredients, the brand quickly gained a reputation for delivering delightful and innovative libations to customers' doorsteps.
